Basics Of Braided Messaging

  • A Strand is a string of Messages that are grouped together.
  • There can be many Strands occurring in parallel, independent of one another.
  • To view a Strand, click any Message in that Strand.
  • To respond on a Strand, send a Message while viewing that Strand.
  • To create a Strand, send a Message while no Strand is selected.

Advanced Techniques

  • To remove a Message from a Strand, hold alt and click the Message. Removing a Message from a Strand turns that Message gray.
  • To prime a gray Message, left click it (certain techniques utilize primed Messages).
  • To add gray Messages to an existing Strand, prime the desired gray Messages, hold alt, and click any Message in the desired Strand.
  • To make a new Strand from a gray Message, hold alt, and click the gray Message (any primed messages will also be included in the new strand).
